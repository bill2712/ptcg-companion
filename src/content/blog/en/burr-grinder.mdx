## What is a Burr Grinder?
The blade grinder is a professional bean grinding equipment compared to the "Blade Grinder". It precisely cuts coffee beans into particles of specified sizes through two steel or ceramic cutters (cone cutters or flat cutters) with specific geometric shapes.

## Why is the cutterhead so important?
The "uniformity" of the grind determines the quality of the extraction. The bean chopper chops randomly like a juicer, producing extremely large particles and extremely fine powder, resulting in both "under-extraction" and "over-extraction" in a cup of coffee. A cutterhead grinder can keep most of the coffee powder particles within the same size range, allowing hot water to treat each powder evenly.

## Conical knife vs flat knife
- **Conical Burrs**: Usually produce a more layered, rounded coffee with a rich taste.
- **Flat Burrs**: Usually produce extremely uniform particles, which can amplify the specific aroma and bright acid value of coffee, and the flavor is very clean and clear.
